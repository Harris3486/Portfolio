Project 1 - Python NBA Top 75 Trivia

Overview - Single question, randomized trivia about the top 75 players in the NBA all-time list.

I wanted to create a trivia containing one question, that can have different answers each loop. I thought to use the list of the top 75 players, and display two types of information to the user: the amount of points scored by a specific player, and the teams they have played for. After doing this 5 times, the loop ends and shows the user how many they got correct out of 5. During the loop, if a user gets a question wrong, it will display who the correct player is. The user can also type in lowercase, or uppercase.

Project 2 - Python Spotify Data Analysis

Overview - Analyzing Spotify personal data using Pandas and Matplotib. To obtain streaming history, download personal data from Spotify USA Inc. Analysis of data pertaining to singular artist, in this case Mac Miller and my personal data. NOTE: My personal data is not uploaded in my repository on Github. You will have to download your own data by reaching out to Spotify to request your data.

I analyzed my data pertaining to music I listened to, but specifically Mac Miller music. I chose this artist because I often listen to him. I was able to produce to different graphs.

The first chart of the top pertains to the frequency of Mac Miller songs I listened to each day. On Tuesday's and Fridays I listened more often. The second chart on the bottom pertains to the frequency of Mac Miller songs I listened to by hour. It makes sense that I didn't listen to music much from 3 AM - 7 AM. I listened the most at 12PM, and often from 9PM-11PM. This project helped me learn how to display data in a visual way, and how to obtain specific information from data.
